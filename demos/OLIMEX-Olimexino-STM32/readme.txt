*****************************************************************************
** ChibiOS/RT port for ARM-Cortex-M3 STM32F103.                            **
*****************************************************************************

** TARGET **

The demo runs on an Olimex Olimexino STM32 board.

** The Demo **

The demo flashes the green and yellow board LEDS using two separate threads.

** Build Procedure **

The demo has been tested by using the free GCC-based toolchain.

** Notes **

Some files used by the demo are not part of ChibiOS/RT but are copyright of
ST Microelectronics and are licensed under a different license.
Also note that not all the files present in the ST library are distributed
with ChibiOS/RT, you can find the whole library on the ST web site:
